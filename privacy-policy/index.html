1. Who We Are

The Bot is developed and operated by nswrld, based in Georgia, United States. For questions about this policy, you can contact us at: nahomn8423@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot processes only minimal data required for its core functionality. We may temporarily process or access the following information:

  • Discord user IDs – to identify command usage or permissions
  • Server (guild) IDs – to store per-server settings or responses
  • Channel IDs – to identify where commands are executed
  • Command inputs – text entered by users (for translations, ምስባክ lookups, or message editing)

We do not permanently store message content, attachments, or personal information beyond what is required to process a specific command.

3. How We Use the Information

We use the information listed above to:

  • Perform translations between Geʽez and English
  • Retrieve daily or weekly ምስባክ readings and psalms

  • Maintain smooth operation and prevent abuse
  • Identify and fix technical issues when errors occur

No collected data is ever sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Data is processed in memory only during a command and not stored permanently.
  • Temporary cached data (e.g., command usage context) may exist only while the bot is running and is cleared automatically after use.
  • When the Bot is removed from a server, all associated cached data and configurations are deleted.

5. Data Sharing
